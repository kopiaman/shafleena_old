About Us Corporate Info

SYNCRAD Sdn Bhd provides the platform for Shafleena Holding towards maximizing localization and being the beneficiary for transfer technology with the ultimate aim of achieving local ‘original equipment manufacturer ‘status. Skilled human resource coupled with competent level of services provided through the specialized program which includes Thru Life Services, Life Extension Program, Technical Training, and Consultations are testimony of client confidence and recognition. Technical aspect and situation evolving within Shafleena Holdings are of Syncrad responsibility.

VISION
To be the leading resource and technical centre in optronics and electronics by the year 2020.

MISSION
To provide world class engineering technology services in the area of optronics and electronics.
